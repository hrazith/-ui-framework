978 Grid System

This is a representation of the 978 grid system.

The Grid is divided into 12 even columns of 54px width.

In Consult Works we break the page up into even columns of multiples of 2, 3, and 4 with a gutter of 30px.

A 4 column layout will have a width of 222px each, dividing up the page into 4 even sections with a 30px gutter in between. (222*4 + 30*3 = 978px)

A 3 column layout will have a width of 306px each, dividing up the page into 3 even sections with a 30px gutter in between. (306*3 + 30*2 = 978px)

To split the layout in half, the page will have a width of 474px each, dividing up the page into half sections with a 30px gutter in between.
