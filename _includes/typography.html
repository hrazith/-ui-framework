Title Bar H1

Financial Planning Fees

These h1 tag title bar headings. They are displayed at the top row of the page, and tend to be the name of the application. We section off the title bar with a <div> and it's own custom "titlebar" class. The h1's are clickable, and link back to the home page of it's application. The syntax and styles are as follows:

  • HTML
  • CSS

                    
<div class="titlebar">
<h1>Financial Planning Fees</h1>
</div>
                    
                

                    
.titlebar h1 {
color: #556696;
padding: 24px 0px 24px 12px;
font-size: 24px;
font-weight: bold;
}
